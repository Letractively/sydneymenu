A Zoyoe account allows you to

  • Create your personal collection of useful local services.
  • Link your personal collection with your facebook via our facebook application.
  • Publish and manage your local service on zoyoe.
  • Use our integration tools to build online community for your local services.

Do not have a Zoyoe account?

  • You can sign into zoyoe via your social account
  • You are also welcomed to register a Zoyoe account which should only takes no more than 3 minutes

Forget your zoyoe password?

  • To identify you account and reset your password, you need to provide the email address you used for registeration.

Already registered, but have not received any mail of activation?

  • Fill the form below to receive a mail that will guide you to activate your account.
